Optimal Quantum Algorithm?

The hope is that we can further improve on the above algorithms, ideally bringing the complexity down to , which equals the output size.

I feel that techniques from quantum triangle finding should give some leverage. In particular the papers {MSS07,LG14} which combine combinatorial insights with quantum walk techniques. Similar to the 3-spanner algorithm, they also pick an initial random subset of nodes, and then focus on finding edges between the neighborhoods of these nodes!
